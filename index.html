OUR SERVICES

General Health Check-up

Regular health check-ups are essential for maintaining your overall well-being. Our comprehensive health check-ups include a thorough physical examination, vital signs assessment, and various diagnostic tests tailored to your needs. Our goal is to detect any potential health issues early and provide you with the necessary guidance to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

  • Wellness package (Male & Female)
  • Comprehensive sexual health check
  • Staff screening package

Home Services

We understand that visiting a healthcare facility is not always convenient. Our home services bring quality healthcare to your doorstep. Book us and our team of qualified professionals can assist with sample collection and you get your results via WhatsApp/E-mail, all in the comfort of your home.

  • Call to book a test
  • Set time and location for sample collection
  • Get all results via WhatsApp or E-mail

Laboratory Services

Accurate and timely laboratory results are crucial for effective diagnosis and treatment. Our state-of-the-art laboratory offers a wide range of tests. We ensure precision and reliability in every test, providing you with fast and accurate results.

  • Chemistry: Liver function, Kidney function, hormonal assay, etc.
  • Hematology: Full blood counts, clotting profile, etc.
  • Microbiology: M.C.S (Urine, HVS, etc.) Malaria Parasite

Blood Banking

Our blood bank is dedicated to ensuring a safe and adequate supply of blood for those in need. We adhere to stringent screening and testing protocols to guarantee the highest quality and safety standards. Whether you are a donor or in need of a blood transfusion, you can trust our blood banking services.

  • As a donor; you get an automatic free Health checkup.
  • Donating blood may help improve your heart health and reduce obesity.
  • You also have the advantage of receiving free blood when you or your family member needs it.

Sonography

Our advanced sonography services utilize high-frequency sound waves to produce detailed images of the body's internal structures. This non-invasive diagnostic tool is essential for monitoring pregnancies, diagnosing medical conditions, and guiding certain medical procedures. Our experienced radiographers ensure comfort and accuracy during your sonography sessions.
